Our Services

United Health Partners unburdens patients with highly specialized needs, taking on the clinical and administrative complexities required to manage complex medical conditions..

Care, Counseling, Coordination

Complex medical conditions often necessitate treatment from multiple health professionals across specializations. The coordination of multiple specialists often falls on the patient, who may also encounter barriers to transportation, comprehension, or financial access. United Health Partners offers care coordination and counseling services to specialty medical patients struggling to navigate the complexities of their care.

Complex Pharmacy Management

Patients with complex medical conditions are often prescribed costly specialty medications that require prior authorizations. United Health Partners equips providers with complex pharmacy management tools, allowing them to provide patients with prior authorizations and support options for out-of-pocket payments.

Clinical Decision Support

As complex medical conditions can necessitate multiple types of providers, United Health Partners works with independent medical practices to assist with clinical decision support, clinical support technology, and up-to-date information on best practices and care standards.

Description of 340B program

The 340B Drug Pricing Program provides financial assistance to medical facilities serving vulnerable communities to increase access to prescription drugs

According to 340B of the Public Health Service Act, healthcare organizations that service many low-income or uninsured patients are eligible for discounted prices on outpatient drugs sold by drug manufacturers participating in Medicaid. Several types of hospitals may qualify including sole community hospitals (SCHs), critical access hospitals (CAHs), rural referral centers (RRCs), and public and nonprofit disproportionate share hospitals (DSH) that treat low-income patients as well as federal grantee organizations. The program was designed to offer outpatient pharmaceuticals at more affordable prices for patients, expanding access to health services to underserved communities and patient populations.
