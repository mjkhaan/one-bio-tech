Our Team

United Health Partners is comprised of specialists from diverse backgrounds, including specialty physicians, scientists, nurses, social workers, software engineers, technologists, lawyers, and business experts. The team is united by the mission to support and strengthen specialty medicine providers serving the most complex and neglected patient populations.

Our Leadership

Ali Karim was trained as an attorney before shifting into the Mergers & Acquisitions group at a multinational investment bank, and then at a global private equity fund focused on healthcare. With an understanding of the difficulties faced by patients with complex medical conditions, Ali founded United Health Partners to resolve these pain points. He holds advanced degrees from the University of Michigan Law School, Columbia University Business School, and The University of Florida, as well as an undergraduate degree in humanities from Georgetown University.
